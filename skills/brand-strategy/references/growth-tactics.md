# Growth Tactics Reference

## Cold Start Playbook (0 to First 100 Followers)

The hardest phase. No algorithm leverage, no social proof, no audience.

### Week 1-2: Establish Presence
1. Optimize profile: clear headshot, positioning statement as bio, pinned post that showcases your best thinking
2. Post daily (even to zero audience -- builds the content library)
3. Follow 50-100 accounts in your niche (not to get followbacks, to study what works)
4. Reply thoughtfully to 10 posts/day from accounts with 1K-50K followers (your future peers)

### Week 3-4: Earn Attention
1. Write 2-3 replies/day that add genuine value (not "great post!" -- actual insight)
2. Create 1 high-effort piece (thread, carousel, article) that showcases your best thinking
3. DM 5 people in your niche with genuine compliments or questions (relationship building)
4. Cross-post your best content to your secondary platform

### The Math
- 10 quality replies/day × 5% follow rate = 3-4 new followers/day
- 1 strong post/day × 1% of impressions follow = depends on reach
- Target: 100 followers in 30 days is realistic with consistent execution

## Growth Phase: 100 to 1,000 Followers

### Engagement Hacking
- **Reply to bigger accounts:** Your replies appear in their followers' feeds
- **Quote tweet/repost with added value:** Adds your take to their audience's attention
- **Engage in first 30 minutes:** Early engagement on popular posts gets you visibility
- **Join X Spaces / LinkedIn Audio:** Speaking builds trust faster than text

### Content Tactics
- **The Thread That Teaches:** Pick your best piece of knowledge, break it into 7-10 tweets. This is the #1 format for follower growth.
- **The Contrarian Take:** Disagree respectfully with a popular opinion in your niche. Provokes engagement.
- **The Curated List:** "10 best [resources] for [audience]." Easy to create, high save/bookmark rate.
- **The Public Learning Log:** Share what you're working on, learning, failing at. Builds investment.

### Collaboration Tactics
- **Shoutout swaps:** Feature someone else, ask them to feature you
- **Co-created content:** Write a thread together, tag each other
- **Guest newsletters:** Write for someone else's newsletter, link back to your profile
- **Podcast/video guesting:** Appear on small podcasts in your niche (easier to book than you think)

## Growth Phase: 1,000 to 10,000 Followers

### Signature Content
At this stage, you need a "thing you're known for":
- A recurring series (e.g., "Monday Market Breakdown")
- A coined term or framework
- A specific content format you own
- A strong opinion you repeat consistently

### Distribution Amplifiers
- **Email list:** Start collecting emails NOW. Social followers are rented. Email subscribers are owned.
- **SEO:** If you have a blog, optimize your best pieces. Long-tail traffic compounds.
- **Repurpose engine:** Every flagship piece becomes 10+ smaller pieces across platforms.
- **Cross-platform leverage:** "I wrote about this in more detail on [other platform]"

### Network Building
- Build genuine relationships with 10-20 creators at your level (your "cohort")
- Engage with each other's content regularly (authentic, not coordinated)
- Share opportunities, introductions, and feedback
- This cohort will grow together and cross-pollinate audiences

## Platform-Specific Growth Tactics

### X / Twitter Growth
- **Golden hour:** Post at 7-8 AM EST, engage heavily for 60 minutes after
- **Thread hooks:** First tweet must be self-contained and compelling even without the thread
- **Bookmark bait:** Include frameworks, templates, or lists people will want to save
- **Profile optimization:** Bio should answer "What will I learn if I follow you?"

### LinkedIn Growth
- **First line is everything:** LinkedIn truncates after ~210 characters. Hook must be there.
- **Carousel dominance:** Document/PDF carousels get 3-5x more reach than text posts
- **Comment strategy:** Leave 3-sentence comments on posts by people with 10K+ followers
- **Connection requests:** Add a personal note referencing their content (80%+ accept rate)

### Reddit Growth (Different Model)
- Reddit does NOT work like X or LinkedIn. You cannot self-promote.
- Growth tactic: Become a genuinely helpful contributor in 2-3 subreddits
- Share expertise freely in comments. Link to your content only when directly relevant.
- Flair, karma, and reputation in a subreddit are the currency.
- Reddit is a top-of-funnel audience research tool, not a direct growth platform.

### Blog/SEO Growth
- **Keyword targeting:** Go after long-tail keywords (4+ words) with low competition
- **Content depth:** 2,000+ words outperforms shorter content for SEO
- **Internal linking:** Every new post links to 2-3 existing posts
- **Update cadence:** Refresh top-performing posts every 6 months with new data
- **Timeline:** SEO takes 3-6 months to show results. Be patient.

### Newsletter Growth
- **Lead magnet:** Offer something valuable in exchange for email (template, guide, checklist)
- **Cross-promote:** Every social post should occasionally mention the newsletter
- **Welcome sequence:** First 3 emails are critical for retention
- **Referral program:** "Share with a friend" can 2-3x growth rate

## Growth Metrics to Track

### Leading Indicators (predict future growth)
- Follower growth rate (week over week %)
- Engagement rate per post (not total engagement)
- Profile visits (people checking you out)
- DMs received (trust signal)
- Saves/bookmarks (content people want to keep)

### Lagging Indicators (confirm past decisions)
- Total followers
- Total impressions/reach
- Newsletter subscriber count
- Website traffic from social
- Inbound opportunities (speaking, collaboration, clients)

### Vanity Metrics (ignore these)
- Likes without comments (passive engagement, low signal)
- Follower count without engagement rate (empty audience)
- Impressions without click-through (eyeballs without action)
- Viral one-off posts (spikes don't build brands, consistency does)
