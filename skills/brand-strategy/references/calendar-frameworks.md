# Content Calendar Frameworks Reference

## The Pillar Rotation System

Rotate content pillars weekly so the audience gets variety but each week
has a clear theme for batch creation.

### 4-Week Rotation (3 Pillars + Personal)
```
Week 1: Pillar 1 (Signature topic - 40%)
Week 2: Pillar 2 (Supporting topic - 25%)
Week 3: Pillar 3 (Supporting topic - 25%)
Week 4: Personal/Mixed (Behind-the-scenes - 10%)
→ Repeat
```

### Content Type Distribution Within Each Week
```
Monday:    TEACH  - Educational, framework, how-to (highest effort, highest value)
Tuesday:   TEACH  - Follow-up or related angle to Monday's topic
Wednesday: SHOW   - Behind-the-scenes, process, results, case study
Thursday:  ASK    - Question, poll, "what do you think?" (engagement driver)
Friday:    SHARE  - Curate someone else's content + your take (network builder)
Weekend:   OFF or lightweight engagement (reply to comments, DMs)
```

## The Content Ladder

Each piece of content exists on a ladder from low-effort to high-effort.
Start at the bottom each week and work up:

```
LEVEL 4: Flagship (1x/month)
  → Long-form article, YouTube video, newsletter deep-dive
  → 3-5 hours to produce
  → Evergreen, searchable, compounding value

LEVEL 3: Core (2-3x/week)
  → Threads, carousels, LinkedIn posts
  → 30-60 min to produce
  → Primary growth drivers

LEVEL 2: Micro (daily)
  → Single tweets, short takes, quotes, observations
  → 5-15 min to produce
  → Maintains presence and algorithm favor

LEVEL 1: Engagement (daily)
  → Replies, comments on others' posts, DMs
  → 15-30 min to produce
  → Builds network and visibility
```

**The repurpose cascade:**
Flagship → Break into 3-4 Core pieces → Break each into 2-3 Micro pieces
One flagship = 2 weeks of content across platforms.

## Time-Budget Templates

### 5 Hours/Week (Minimum Viable Brand)
```
Mon: 60 min - Write 1 core post + 2 micro posts
Tue: 30 min - Engagement (replies, comments on 10 posts)
Wed: 60 min - Write 1 core post + 2 micro posts
Thu: 30 min - Engagement + respond to your comments
Fri: 30 min - Write 1 share post + schedule next week
Sat: 30 min - Engagement only
Sun: OFF
```

### 8 Hours/Week (Growth Mode)
```
Mon: 90 min - Write 1 flagship or 2 core posts
Tue: 60 min - Write 1 core post + engagement
Wed: 60 min - Repurpose Mon content for secondary platform
Thu: 60 min - Write 1 ask/engagement post + replies
Fri: 60 min - Write 1 share post + schedule + weekly review
Sat: 30 min - Engagement only
Sun: OFF
```

### 12 Hours/Week (Aggressive Growth)
```
Mon: 120 min - Flagship content creation
Tue: 90 min - 2 core posts + engagement (30 min)
Wed: 90 min - Repurpose for secondary platform + engagement
Thu: 60 min - Ask/engagement content + community interaction
Fri: 60 min - Share + curate + schedule all next week
Sat: 60 min - Engagement + DM relationship building
Sun: 60 min - Weekly review + plan next week's theme
```

## The Hook Bank System

For each content pillar, maintain a running list of hooks. When it's time
to write, pick from the bank rather than staring at a blank page.

### Hook Formulas That Work for Personal Brands

**The Contrarian:** "Most people think [common belief]. Here's why that's wrong:"
**The Number:** "[X] things I learned after [experience]. Thread:"
**The Story:** "In 2019, I [specific event]. It changed how I think about [topic]."
**The Question:** "Why do [audience] keep [making this mistake]?"
**The Result:** "I [did specific thing] and [got specific result]. Here's how:"
**The Curation:** "The best [resource] I've found on [topic] this week:"
**The Framework:** "My [name] framework for [outcome]. Works every time:"
**The Hot Take:** "[Topic] is broken. Here's what nobody's saying:"
**The Before/After:** "6 months ago I [before state]. Now I [after state]. What changed:"
**The Mistake:** "The biggest mistake I made with [topic] (and what I do instead):"

## Calendar Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- **Posting without a pillar:** Random topics confuse the audience about what you stand for
- **All teach, no personality:** Audience follows people, not textbooks
- **Front-loading effort:** Most creators burn out in week 2. Start sustainable, add later
- **Ignoring engagement days:** Posting without replying is broadcasting, not building
- **No repurpose plan:** Creating net-new content for every platform is unsustainable
- **Rigid calendar:** The calendar is a guide, not a prison. Timely content can override it
